Built an LLM-based information synthesis pipeline for real-time extraction and summarization from papers, articles, repositories, and technical news sources. The goal was to create an autonomous workflow that could continuously gather information, cluster related content, and produce useful summaries for fast-moving technical domains.

  <li>Summarization</li>
  <li>Embeddings</li>
  <li>Evaluation</li>
</ul>

* Used Llama 3, Qwen, and GPT-OSS in adaptive summarization workflows.
* Designed chunking, embedding, and semantic clustering strategies for long-document processing.
* Led collaboration with a 6-member research team and evaluated against ROUGE and semantic similarity metrics.
* Designed workflows for long-document processing, source aggregation, and comparative summarization.
* Connected research evaluation metrics with practical user-facing information synthesis needs.
